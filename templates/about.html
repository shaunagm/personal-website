About Me


I'm a programmer and a writer, a researcher and a community manager, and a lot of other things besides. I tend to work at the intersection of people and technology and I have a soft spot in my heart for anything open: open science, open government, open source software.

I currently run a technology consulting business, Galaxy Rise Consulting. I work primarily with individuals and small organizations to address their technical needs, whether that means building websites and plugins, analyzing datasets, or documenting messy code.

I serve on the Board of Directors of Tech Inquiry, a non-profit run by tech workers combatting problems and abuses in the tech industry. I also co-organize the DC chapter of the Tech Workers Coalition.

I'm always interested in potential collaborations, so if you have an idea or an opportunity, please get in touch.
