About

I'm an independent researcher and developer who focuses on open technologies and communities. I am fascinated by the way we humans share information with each other - what we learn, who we trust, how we decide.

I run a technology business, Galaxy Rise Consulting. I do web and mobile development for clients, and offer data science and technical writing services as well.

In the past, I've been a social psychology researcher and the manager of an open source outreach non-profit. I've compiled meta-analyses of medical research for patients, written novels and children's books, and even wrangled monkeys. To learn more about me and my varied past, check out my CV/portfolio.

I'm always interested in potential collaborations, so if you have an idea or an opportunity, please get in touch.
